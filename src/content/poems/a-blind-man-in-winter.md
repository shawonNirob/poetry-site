---
title: A Blind Man in Winter
date: 2025-12-29
excerpt: A train, a loyal pet, and rain finding a face that cannot see it coming.
---
Over a long, long place,
From the mountains a train rushes,
Night not over, yet he listens.
A faithful pet, tail swaying slow,
Looking at him with quiet eyes,
Pressing its head against his leg.
On a winter morning,
A blind man sits beneath the sun,
Warm rays resting on his skin.
When clouds gather in the sky,
A million raindrops fall from above,
Touching his face with gentle rhythm,
Sliding down his skin to the ground.
