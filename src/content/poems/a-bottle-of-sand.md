---
title: A Bottle of Sand
date: 2026-01-02
excerpt: The sun, the tea, the wine, the return, and two grains that never quite mix.
---
Plato sees the sun outside the cave.
Zen brings the warm tea.
Rumi shares the wine—
a whole sea of wine.
Ibn Arabi shows the realm.
Let the sun pass through his skin,
touch the tea in the morning,
sit by the sea's edge,
drink the wine,
drink, drunk, and be overwhelmed,
read the book of the realm,
and return to the cave,
carrying nothing but himself,
walking the thin line between two worlds—
holding them like a bottle of sand,
a mixture of black and white grains that touch,
but never mix.
