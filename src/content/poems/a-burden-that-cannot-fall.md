---
title: A Burden That Cannot Fall
date: 2026-04-28
excerpt: Some things pull downward with everything they have, and still don't fall.
---
Sometimes,
there are things in this universe
that I cannot share,
cannot tell,
cannot write down,
and cannot even bear to hear myself.
As if they are a burden that cannot fall,
yet longs to fall,
pulling downward with immense force,
but still cannot fall.
