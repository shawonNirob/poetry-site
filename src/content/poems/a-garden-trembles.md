---
title: A Garden Trembles
date: 2026-05-10
excerpt: Everything is sealed shut, and still something inside keeps blooming.
---
My mouth is sealed,
yet inside it, a garden trembles.
A thousand unspoken words
bloom like roses in the dark.
No one sees them, not even me.
My hands are bound,
but the rope cannot touch me.
My body has flesh,
but time does not pass.
My room has a corridor,
but I cannot walk through it.
Out there, a wind calls my name,
not in language, but in silence.
I ask what is happening,
and the question is lost
before it reaches the air.
