---
title: Ah, Soul
date: 2026-03-11
excerpt: Searching through fog and bloodstream alike, and never quite getting lost.
---
I search through the city,
into the fog of every dream,
beneath the morning sunlight.
And you came here,
into my bloodstream,
in every breath I take,
through the air that touches my fingers,
into the void that surrounds it all.
Ah, soul, you are not lost,
never, ever.
Maybe you are layering all things.
Maybe you do not know.
Maybe you do not need to know.
And that is exactly who you are.
