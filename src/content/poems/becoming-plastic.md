---
title: Becoming Plastic
date: 2026-05-26
excerpt: Crying to disappear, and becoming something that never quite does.
---
I cry and cry.
I cry to vanish,
to be erased from the world.
But the crying changes nothing.
I do not disappear.
Instead, I become every cry
that has ever cried.
I became plastic,
demanding standing.
Death became an illusion to the plastic,
for it only melts
and reforms again.
And I am unable to touch it,
unable to cry,
unable to show it the way to vanish.
