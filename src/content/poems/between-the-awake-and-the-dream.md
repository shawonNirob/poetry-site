---
title: Between the Awake and the Dream
date: 2025-10-22
excerpt: A traveler passing through the gates of sleep, watched by a calm and endless sea.
---
Don't go, don't go.
Don't sleep, don't sleep.
It comes before sleep.
You are not asleep; you are the traveler,
Moving from time to time,
Passing through the gates of sleep.
When everything falls, look back -
The great calm sea is watching you,
Between the awake and the dream.
Space has a territory of its own kind,
Like the work within an alchemist's chamber,
Where silver turns to gold.
Like stars that are born and return to their end,
Clouds that cross the sky,
Rain that touches the ground,
And flowers that turn toward the sun.
The map of that territory lies within this line.
Do not be only the traveler who passes by—
Look into the depths.
Do not fall; see how it falls.
See how all things return to the One.
