---
title: Clay and Ruh
date: 2025-12-25
excerpt: The same particle running through everything, waiting to be accepted.
---
He was made of clay,
The clay no different from gold.
The same particle, taking many forms;
At the core, the same essence runs through the universe.
The clay returns to clay,
The ruh returns to Him.
When he accepts this, all things fall away.
All suffering falls away; he steps out of the fire.
And if he is not here, the earth will be lonely.
The air moves through shadows, looking for him.
The moon is alone, unable to show him its beauty.
The sky is lost, unable to reveal its glory.
The universe forgets itself when he is not here to write the equation.
The Truth hiding within the Truth,
Where even the idea of Liberation fades,
And all things disappear into a single point.
