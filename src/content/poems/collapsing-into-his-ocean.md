---
title: Collapsing Into His Ocean
date: 2025-11-19
excerpt: An illusion dissolving like smoke, into a fire that doesn't burn.
---
Ah, illusion, I have seen you.
You're collapsing,
collapsing like smoke vanishes into the skies,
collapsing as if you never existed.
Falling,
falling,
into His Ocean,
into His Fire,
the Fire that does not burn,
the Water that is not cold.
Form, His.
Formless, His.
Peace, His.
Only He remains.
Fire has returned
to its place,
in the Realm of Imagination,
not Reality.
