---
title: Die Before You Die
date: 2025-11-27
excerpt: Cleaning a half-cleaned glass, and finding Eternity already shining through it.
---
One day he will begin to clean the glass,
and suddenly Eternity will shine.
Light melts the glass he has only half-cleaned.
The Angel of Death appears,
not to take him, but to remind him
who he truly is:
in His Imagination, not in Reality.
And the moment he accepts death,
his foot moves forward
into the place before birth and after death.
And in that moment, he stops dying.
He is like a bubble
born on the surface of the sea,
the whole sky enters that bubble,
sinking into the deep, vanishing into the water,
and its breath returning to the sky.
