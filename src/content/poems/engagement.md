---
title: Engagement
date: 2026-03-23
excerpt: Choosing not to engage, and slowly becoming the background instead.
---
You find engagement difficult,
because engagement requires energy you do not have.
So you choose not to engage,
to become, by default,
someone who lives in simplicity.
But then the difficulty begins,
and that default turns out to be something entirely different.
The world itself requires you to engage with it.
Even water requires engagement to drink it.
It is not simple.
It may be one of the most difficult things in this universe.
All lifeless things ask you to engage,
not asking, but forcing.
And the force is so powerful that you have no other option.
So you choose to engage.
But you are not truly engaging.
You withdraw.
A separation begins.
A middle line appears.
You move into the background
and become lifeless.
The lifeless things become alive
and move around you,
as if they need you for their purpose,
for the task they serve,
for the visibility they perform.
And you are merely something they require
to keep them alive.
