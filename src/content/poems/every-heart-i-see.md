---
title: Every Heart I See
date: 2025-11-11
excerpt: Searching the galaxy above, and finding the same light in every gaze below.
---
I seek Him in the above,
Within the endless galaxy.
Yet when I look down, I find Him
In every heart, in every gaze.
Those eyes, how could I ever forget?
My heart longs to be given for this.
Though it is not mine to keep,
It belongs to Him, the True Owner.
Let Buddha wake up inside me,
Let the Prophet rise,
Let Jesus live in my heart.
Why live with this fragile heart of mine,
When every heart I see
Breathes His presence,
Shines with His light,
Whispers His name?
