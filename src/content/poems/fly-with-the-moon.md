---
title: Fly With the Moon
date: 2025-12-01
excerpt: A short ascent through cloud and doubt, into a sky with no ground to fall to.
---
Step up, Let's fly,
fly through the clouds into the sky,
fly inside the heart, in the frequency of love,
fly with the moon, beyond the golden light,
into the midst of the dancing birds,
in the silence of space.
The world sleeps,
and morning light passes beneath the sea.
The doubt in his mind,
see, it rises and flies too.
There is no ground; he will never fall.
Only the vast sky and the soul.
