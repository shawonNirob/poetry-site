---
title: How Desperate I Am
date: 2026-04-24
excerpt: Looking for meaning in a universe that never asked to be understood.
---
I search for the meaning of living
and find that the universe does not care about me.
Yet I search for meaning within it,
how desperate I am.
When I, too, should not care about it,
since everything is temporary,
nothing returns from nothing,
as if only balance remains
that is how it works.
