---
title: I Became My Wish
date: 2026-05-18
excerpt: When you stop wanting to speak, something starts speaking for you.
---
I don't want to speak.
I don't want to write.
I don't even want to read a sentence,
because I do not know how words are pronounced.
For how long, I do not know.
From how far, I do not know.
Time passes.
One day, I became my wish.
Someone starts writing me down,
whispers before my eyes,
speaks inside my ears,
singing a melody in the air.
