---
title: I Lie So Truly
date: 2026-06-03
excerpt: Love, lies, and the birds that sing them anyway.
---
I lie to you
about how love cannot be a rescue,
yet I only love you,
because I am unable to tell you the truth.
I lie to myself
about the way love flows endlessly,
the way the world moves with the clouds,
passing me by,
without noticing that I no longer look.
I lie so truly
even how the birds sing the lie,
how lies move through everything,
until I become the lie.
Still, you cannot see me,
because I live inside you.
