---
title: Not Special
date: 2026-06-11
excerpt: On existing only from a certain angle, and never from your own.
---
You're not special,
not because you are not,
but because you do not exist.
Where I am unable to think of you,
unable to think of me,
of the world,
or of the horizon.
Yet the world exists,
and so does the horizon,
and so do you.
There, you are special,
and I do not exist,
because I am you.
