---
title: Only the Dream
date: 2026-04-16
excerpt: Loving someone completely, but only inside the one place they exist.
---
I live only in my dream.
There, I love you most.
If neither I nor my dream exists,
then nothing would remain.
But I exist, and so does the dream
where I love you
as if I love nothing else,
not even me,
only the dream
where you exist.
