---
title: Scheduled Visit
date: 2026-06-27
excerpt: Running from a scream that never stops, until the world starts over without you.
---
I run and run,
from myself,
from the scream
of the world screaming inside me.
But that running doesn't work,
because I am the hell thrown onto Earth.
And I am unable to stop it,
unable to touch it.
The more I try to touch it,
the farther it moves away.
So I only listen,
listen to the scream of the world.
Then, one day,
without any notification,
Earth stopped moving around the Sun,
my mouth became shut,
my eyes became blind,
my ears became deaf,
my heart vanished,
as if I had never been here,
as if only a version of me had come to visit.
Then many years passed,
how many, I cannot count.
I was born again on Earth,
as if for a scheduled visit.
The world arranges itself again,
but I remain the same.
