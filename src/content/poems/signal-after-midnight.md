---
title: Signal After Midnight
date: 2026-07-01
excerpt: A small blue light left on, standing in for everyone still awake.
---
After the last song,
the earphones,
before sleep,
I plug them in to charge.
And each night,
I do not pray,
praying became something
I forgot long ago.
As if everything had already fallen silent,
as if the world itself had gone to sleep,
as if I knew
I would never wake again.
Because I cannot enter sleep,
I begin waiting
to break the world's sleep.
But sleep is peaceful,
so peaceful it lasts too long,
endlessly long,
until it forgets me,
leaving me outside it,
while I still live inside.
