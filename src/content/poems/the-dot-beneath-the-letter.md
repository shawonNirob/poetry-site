---
title: The Dot Beneath the Letter
date: 2026-02-23
excerpt: Everything contained inside the smallest, quietest point of a text.
---
The entire universe is contained in the Qur'an.
The Qur'an is contained in the first chapter.
The chapter is contained in the first sentence.
The sentence is contained in the first letter.
And the letter is contained in the dot beneath it.
Ah, soul, you are that dot.
The tiny dot.
The tiny seed.
Yet within it stands a massive tree,
with millions of leaves, fruits, and forests.
Animals move within that forest.
The ground spreads beneath them.
An entire galaxy circles around that ground.
