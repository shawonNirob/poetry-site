---
title: The Most Beautiful Dream
date: 2026-04-04
excerpt: A soft, floating world that turns hostile the moment it's noticed.
---
Because nothing is permanent,
yet nothing is temporary.
Where he plays his role,
he lets himself play.
The earth itself moves with it,
as if within a long, sweet dream.
The wide air,
and the scent mingled within it,
make the world so soft
that he seems to float within it.
Then the permanent returns,
taking control.
But the earth offers no support,
it protects the dream
and rejects him,
as if he were a threat
to the floating air,
to the most beautiful dream,
beyond which no other dream exists.
