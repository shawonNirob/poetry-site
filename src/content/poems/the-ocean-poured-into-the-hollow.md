---
title: The Ocean Poured Into the Hollow
date: 2025-12-17
excerpt: Asking for a wound to be fixed, and receiving far more than the cry asked for.
---
I asked Him,
Ya Allah, a wound lies open in my heart.
It keeps draining the water out of me;
the earth's pulsing lifeblood is fading away.
Fix this, or take me back to You, please.
And what grace did He show?
He did not only listen.
He did not only mend it.
He did more than my cry asked for.
He brought the entire ocean
and poured it into the hollow,
then sealed it with warmth,
as only the Almighty can—
Al-Rahim, the Most Merciful,
Al-Qahhar, the Irresistible Dominator,
The Restorer of my fractured whole.
