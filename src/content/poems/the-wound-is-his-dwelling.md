---
title: The Wound Is His Dwelling
date: 2025-12-21
excerpt: A question to Ibn Arabi about a wound, and an answer that turns it into a home.
---
"Why is there a wound in my heart?
Why does water keep leaking out of me?"
If you asked Ibn Arabi, master of masters, greatest sheikh,
He would answer:
"Ah, so you have finally seen it.
It is not a wound.
It is His dwelling place.
The water you feel is returning to its Source.
The dam is breaking.
The barrier you built around your heart is collapsing.
Watch how the water flows in its own direction,
not yours.
Watch how it rises,
how it floods everything you once called 'the world,'
how it washes the earth clean,
how it climbs toward the moon.
Now watch the earth dissolve into that water,
watch the heavens dissolve,
watch the whole universe return to the One Ocean.
The One Timeless Ocean,
Only His Ocean,
Nothing else."
