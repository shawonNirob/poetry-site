---
title: There Is Only You
date: 2026-06-15
excerpt: Trying to explain something and losing the words to it completely.
---
What should I tell you
about the thing that you own?
I should tell you.
I should tell you that I am unable to tell.
Because the moment I begin,
something strange happens,
the world vanishes,
leaving only you.
I try again,
but my voice never becomes words.
Instead, the words arrange themselves
into the shape you expect.
The words begin telling you,
telling the world,
not because I want them to,
but because I cannot stop them.
So I only wish I could show you.
What could I show?
There is only you.
