---
title: Whatever You Do
date: 2026-06-19
excerpt: A short vow of company, however things turn out.
---
If you're happy, I am with you.
Maybe you're sad,
maybe you're alone, I am with you.
If you're going mad,
or craving more,
or erasing everything, I remain with you.
Whatever you do, you do perfectly.
Then one day you die,
and become me.
