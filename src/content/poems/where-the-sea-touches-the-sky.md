---
title: Where the Sea Touches the Sky
date: 2025-10-26
excerpt: A city's noise collapsing into the sky, and every traffic light becoming a star.
---
Eyes do not see; the seen does not arise.
Where the sea touches the sky,
millions of fish rise toward the heights,
swimming to the sky until stars become their eyes.
Millions of birds descend from the sky to the sea,
flying down to hold the fish in their beaks.
Countless creatures gather beneath a single light by the roadside,
as if they have found the source.
Every traffic light in the crowded city becomes a star in the galaxy.
The noise of the busiest city collapses into the vast symphony of the sky.
The road becomes a river flowing through eternity,
concrete turns to mountains,
opening space for life.
And earth becomes the ground where the eternal unfolds.
The great silence leaves the sky
and comes to settle in your heart.
The great presence fills the mountain
and echoes through the world.
The great compassion, from Its throne,
offers the heart of the universe,
for all unfolds by His command.
Then why hold on to stories and thoughts?
See how they rise and dissolve into the sky.
