---
title: Where You Are Alone
date: 2026-04-20
excerpt: A long night of trying to look busy enough to not notice the silence.
---
In the long night,
fog gathers in the distance.
Everything tries to sleep after a tired day,
so I try to sleep,
sleep as if I no longer exist,
sleep as if I should not have to exist.
But I cannot sleep.
Sleep does not exist.
So I sit,
keeping myself busy to shatter the sorrow I carry,
keeping myself busy to pretend I am not alone,
keeping myself busy to find a place where I can hide.
But that place does not exist,
where You are alone,
and I am.
