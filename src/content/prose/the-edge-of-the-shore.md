---
title: The Edge of the Shore
date: 2025-10-18
excerpt: One foot on solid ground, one foot from the sea, waiting for the fear to grow small enough.
---
At the edge of the shore, one foot rests on the solid ground, one foot inches from the great sea. Waves roar. Heart yearns to touch water. Air tastes of salt, yet the foot does not move. It fears dissolving, fears losing itself.

Days pass. The ground grows hotter. The foot can no longer hold. Universe leans closer, waiting. Then comes the moment of grace, the heat on the shore grows so great that the fear of the unknown sea becomes smaller than the pain of staying on familiar ground.

And in that timeless moment, the sea reaches up, waves bending like hands, whispering; stars begin their melody, and the cosmos begins to dance with the fabric of its own being.
