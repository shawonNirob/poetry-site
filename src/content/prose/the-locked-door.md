---
title: The Locked Door
date: 2025-10-14
excerpt: A door at the deepest edge of the heart, and a key that turns out to be inside it.
---
At the deepest part of the heart, at its very edges, there is a layer.

That layer has a door, and the door has a lock.

They have been telling their story in the wind for a thousand years.

They invite you to see the door.

You have reached the end of the heart and now stand before it.

But the door is locked. Steel guards it, there is no way to break through.

You need the key. But where is the key? Where will you find it?

The key, it comes from the stars,

From the wind by the riverside, from the raft of knowledge.

Or, if not, look through the wound,

Gather the broken pieces of the heart.

The key lies within.

Open the door, the light has been waiting for you for eternity.

Open your eyes, see the unimaginable light.
