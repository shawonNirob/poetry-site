---
title: Three Ways to Live
date: 2025-10-10
excerpt: Chasing the future, holding the past, or walking the line between, until none of it matters.
---
They said, "There are three ways to live. The first is to chase the future, reaching for dreams, success, love, or whatever lies ahead. The second is to live in the past, holding on to knowledge, memories, and what they have taught you. The third is to walk between the two, the very thin line of the present, letting both guide you in balance.

But when all three are let go, it is discovered that there is no way to live at all, life simply lives itself. Leaves move slowly through the air. Tea holds its quiet warmth. The wind touches the skin. Rain falls. Breath rises. Life simply is, raw and unfiltered, the way of the universe."
